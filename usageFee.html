Registration Guide

※ For tickets with restricted entry dates, present your commuter pass and ID at the ticket office when visiting on the restricted date. You can purchase a one-day pass at a 40% discount from the regular price for that day.

※ Precautions when signing up for 365 Senior/365 Baby

  • - Submission of documentary evidence proving that you are eligible for subscription is required (when paying for Smart Reservation, you must choose to pay on-site)
  • - Baby confirmation documents: copy of resident registration, health insurance card Senior confirmation documents: resident registration copy, driver's license, passport However, when re-registering, you can sign up without separate confirmation in Smart Reservation.
  • - You can sign up for the Baby Pass once in your life, and if you cancel it before expiration, you cannot re-register with that type of pass.
Preferential information

※ Preferential treatment cannot be applied in conjunction with other affiliated cards and promotions

※ If ID/document verification is required, it can only be purchased at the on-site ticket office.

Preferential treatment according to age (presentation of ID or documents that can confirm age)
  • - Seniority: 65 years of age or older
  • - Youth: 13 to 18 years old
  • - Child : 36 months ~ 12 years old
  • - Less than 36 months: Free use (separate purchase of ticket required for paid facilities)
  • ※ Children's pass: 6,000 won (available for Bung-Bung Car, Robot Car, and Automobile Kingdom)
  • ※ Adult rates are combined with youth, refer to 'Rate & Promotion > Special Promotion' for youth promotions
Preferential price for the disabled (Present the certificate of registration for the disabled)
  • - People with severe disabilities
    Person: Preferential treatment applied
    Accompanying person: Preferential treatment applied (up to 1 person)
  • - Disabled persons with mild disabilities
    Person: Preferential treatment applied
  • ※ One-day pass can be purchased at preferential price
Preference for national meritorious persons (presentation of certificates)
  • - Person: You can purchase a one-day pass at a 50% preferential price
  • - Accompanying person: 50% preferential price for one-day pass (up to 1 person)
Preferential treatment for families with 3 or more children (Present proof of family with 3 or more children, such as health insurance card, certified copy)
  • - A one-day pass can be purchased at a preferential price.
  • - Preferential treatment: Couples with three or more children and direct children with the youngest child under the age of 15
  • - Benefits apply even when only some family members in the household use it
Pregnant women preferential treatment (Present maternity handbook or pregnancy confirmation)
  • - One-day pass (pregnant women) can be purchased at preferential price
