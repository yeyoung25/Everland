AMENNITIES

Facilities
  • No pets allowed
    Except for guide dogs for visually handicapped and hearing-impaired persons
    A separate place for pets is not available
  • Handicapped Parking Area
    Handicapped parking spaces are prepared at each parking lot at MA, MB, and number 1~5
  • Information on using Samchully CNG charging station
    Operation: 365 days a year, 24 hours operation
    Inquiries: 070-4647-0117
    CNG cars and buses can be charged
    Customer waiting room, electric vehicle chargers (7 units) in operation
  • Guide to using the electric vehicle charger
    Cabin Hostel parking lot (3) * 1 fast, 2 slow
    Group bus parking lot (14) * 8 express, 6 slow
    CNG filling station (7) * 5 fast, 2 slow
  • Hynet Everland Hydrogen Refueling Station Information
    Operation: Tue~Sun 08:00~20:00 *Closed every Monday
    Inquiries: 010-3779-9289 (Director Park Jong-hyun)
    Cost: 8,800 won/kg
  • Stroller rental (under 24 months)
    Sky Cruise (opposite the top)
    First come, first served basis
    Single strollers : KRW 5,000 (deposit KRW 1,000, 16kg or less)
    Kids wagon : B - KRW 30,000, C - KRW 25,000 (deposit KRW 50,000 / 50kg or less)
    locking device : KRW 5,000 (deposit KRW 3,000)
    Electric stroller : B - KRW 70,000, C - KRW 50,000 (deposit KRW10,000)
  • Baby service
    Baby Service Alpine (Safari World Right)
    Baby Service Four Seasons (Next to China Moon)
    Baby Service Aesop Village (Inside Aesop Village)
    Baby Service Sky Cruise (opposite the top of Sky Cruise)
    ※ It is subject to change depending on the operation situation.
  • Wheelchair and electric wheelchair rental fee
    Sky Cruise (opposite the top)
    It is rented first to guests with disabilities who are unable to walk and on a first-come, first-served basis.
    Check your phone on site and rent it.
    General: 3,000 won (free of charge when presenting a disability registration card)
    Electricity: KRW 5,000 (Only for the disabled / presentation of a disability registration card)
  • Locker
    It is detailed in the GUIDE booklet on site.
    Only 500 won coins are available
    Small: KRW 1,500 / Medium, large KRW 2,000
    Please contact the stroller desk (031-320-9079) to use the cargo carrier storage box
  • A medical office
    Front door medical office 031-320-9082
    Alpine Medical Center 031-320-9711
    Nurse resident, Automatic Defibrillator (AED) in place
  • Ambulance
    Hospital evacuation in case of emergency
    Residence of first aid workers
    031-320-9119
  • Automatic Defibrillator (AED)
    Place at various park points
    031-320-9119
  • A first-aid bag
    Park at various points
    Articles (disposable bands, disinfectants, etc.) ointment For ointment, visit the medical office.
    031-320-511
  • Gondola and lift
    Sky Cruise: (down) in front of Kids Recovery, (up) in front of Bumper Car.
    Human Sky: Next to (down) Oriental Restaurant, in front of (up) Tiger Valley (not operated in case of bad weather)
    Skyway: (down) in front of the Rolling X Train, (up) Skyway Gift Shop (not operated in case of bad weather)
  • ATM
    Across from Sky Cruise (top), next to Woori Bank, China Moon, next to Information, Kookmin Card Application Center.
  • Management of acquisitions/finding lost children
    Customer Service Office, Alpine Ticket Office
    031-320-5050
    You can check the acquired items on the Lost 112 site of the National Police Agency.
    Access http://www.lost112.go.kr or use the mobile app LOST112.
  • Unmanned auxiliary battery rental fee
    Only auxiliary batteries can be rented. (1500 won per hour)
    It will be charged up to 36,000 won, and there may be an additional charge if you do not return it within 120 hours.
    You can rent/return it at any charging pig installation store across the country.
    (You can check the installation point through the charging pig app)
    If there is something wrong with the battery you rented, you can get a refund if you return it within 3 minutes.
    Rechargeable Pig Customer Center 1644-5773, Kakao Talk @Rechargeable Pig
  • Restriction on carrying in for safety
    Mats (excluding single-seat cushions), burners, small rides (bicycles, inline, kickboards, etc.), wirelessly controlled rides
  • Please refrain from harming others or dangerous behavior
    Damage to the grass/flower garden, cutting in line, singing loudly, violent behavior, using attractions after drinking, lying on the bench, feeding animals or filming with flash on, burning children's throats, etc.
    Please use the lunch box only in the picnic area.
  • Please respect the workers who do their best.
    Please refrain from speaking informally, swearing, or physical contact even if there are any mistakes or deficiencies.
    Please call the manager and let me know your suggestions. (Representative number: 031-320-5000)
  • Cautions for Smoking
    Everland is designated as a non-smoking park to protect against secondhand smoke and fire hazards. A fine of 50,000 won will be imposed under the National Health Promotion Act, so if you want to smoke, please use the designated smoking place (refer to the guide map).
