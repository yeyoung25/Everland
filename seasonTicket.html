Season ticket
  • 365 Standard

    Availability date : 365 days a year

    A new subscription fee : Adult 260,000 won / Child 220,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : Adult 240,000 won / Child 180,000 won

    Age standard : Adults 13 to 59 years old, Children 3 to 12 years old ※ Based on the starting date of the commuter pass

  • 350 Standard

    Availability date : About 350 days a year excluding the peak season

    A new subscription fee : Adult 240,000 won / Child 190,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : Adult 190,000 won / Child 140,000 won

    Age standard : Adults 13 to 59 years old, Children 3 to 12 years old※ Based on the starting date of the commuter pass

  • 320 Select

    Availability date : About 320 days a year excluding peak season

    A new subscription fee : Adult 190,000 won / Child 150,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : Same type Sign up for a new rate or 365/350 standard tickets re-entry possible

    Age standard : Adults 13 to 59 years old, Children 3 to 12 years old※ Based on the starting date of the commuter pass

  • 250 Week Day

    Availability date : Approximately 250 weekdays excluding weekends/holidays/sandwich days of the year

    A new subscription fee : Adult 140,000 won / Child 110,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : Same type Sign up for a new rate or 365/350 standard tickets re-entry possible

    Age standard : Adults 13 to 59 years old, Children 3 to 12 years old※ Based on the starting date of the commuter pass

  • 365 Senior

    Availability date : 365 days a year

    A new subscription fee : 130,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : 130,000 won

    Age standard : 60 years old more

  • 365 babies

    Availability date : 365 days a year

    A new subscription fee : 90,000 won

    Within 1 year after maturity Fee upon re-enrolment : Only in different types re-entry possible

    Age standard : 24 to 35 months※ 36 month birthday until the day before Issuance (= start) possible

Information by type of commuter pass
  • 365 Standard / 350 Standard / 320 Select / 250 Weekday
    - Please check the availability calendar. Please check the availability calendar.
    - On restricted days, the mobile app's commuter pass is disabled.
    - If you wish to use Everland on a restricted day, you must purchase a one-day pass separately.
    - You can get a 40% discount on your own pass by presenting the deactivated commuter pass at the on-site ticket counter at the time of purchase.
    - A separate fee is incurred when issuing a physical pass card, and the pass number is changed. (cost 2,000 won)
  • 365 Senior
    - When signing up, please present your resident registration card or driver's license to verify your age.
    - When re-registering for the same guest, you can sign up without checking any documents in Smart Reservation.
  • 365 baby
    - When signing up, please present a copy of your resident registration or health insurance card that can verify your age. Subscription gift (drink cup + carbonated drink or bottled water) Pick up location: Café Jungle Camp, Alps Kuche, Alpine, Magic Time
    - 365 Baby tickets can be subscribed only once in a lifetime, and if canceled before expiration, re-registration with 365 Baby tickets is not possible.
Common Information
  • - When issuing a physical commuter pass, a separate fee is incurred and the pass number is changed. (cost 2,000 won)
  • - Everland's operating hours and festivals vary by period, so please check the website and mobile app before visiting.
  • - The age-specific amount of each commuter pass is applied based on the start date of the commuter pass.
    1. Example) For guests born on February 1, 2010, child rates will be applied from the start of January 31, 2023, and adult rates will be applied from the start of February 1, 2023.
    2. For guests born on February 1, 63, adult rates will be applied starting on January 31, 2023, and senior rates will be applied starting on February 1, 2023.
