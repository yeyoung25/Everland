RECOMMENDED COURSE

Recommended coursefor enjoying attractions
Visiting attractions and performances are subject to change depending on weather conditions and season. Please check suspension and waiting time information on the Everland APP.

  • 01 Rainy weather recommended course

    1. Championship Rodeo

    Enjoy a dance party, spinning around and around with an array of catchy songs. Who will be the best rodeo rider ever? Be a rodeo champion, and enjoy a tough rodeo riding experience!

  • 01 Rainy weather recommended course

    2. Panda World

    You’re cordially invited to Korea’s only exhibition hall that incorporates advanced IT, where you can not only view but also experience panda. Diverse IT devices deliver a vivid experience of a panda’s everyday life. A space where you can view the cute panda right under your nose! Place : Panda World

  • 01 Rainy weather recommended course

    3. Sea Lion Village Animal Talk

    Sea Lion Village found after visiting Panda World. Zootopia has a program called 'Animal Talk' that shows Sea Lion, Penguin, Tiger, etc. at a specific time. Sea Lion Village was a space where we could see a fun show of animals we know as seals.

  • 01 Rainy weather recommended course

    4. Super Wings Animal Talk

    Meet a variety of pretty birds! Colorful talking parrots greet you.

  • 01 Rainy weather recommended course

    5. Safari World

    An open safari view spread out through a wide window Closer, a chance to meet a live predator right under your nose! Lower vision, closer vision, wider vision. Meet more vivid wildtrams.

    1. Flying Rescue

    Go and rescue Limo the Lion on the flying device. The mission is to rescue Limo, who is stuck on a tower. Enjoy the mini drop that goes up and down! A person of 90~100cm must be accompanied by a guardian.

  • 02 Recommended course for children

    2. Lily Dance

    When a mommy frog begins to sing a song for her baby frog, beautiful lotus flowers spin round and round. Spin the wheel inside the lotus flower. The faster you spin the wheel, the faster your speed will be. A person of 100cm or less must be accompanied by a guardian.

  • 02 Recommended course for children

    3. Play Yard
