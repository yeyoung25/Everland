How to use Everland
For safe and enjoyable use, the following items are restricted.
- Burner/Mat (excluding cushions for one person)/Pets (excluding guide dogs)
- Small boarding devices such as bicycles/inline/kickboards, radio-controlled boarding devices
Please refrain from actions that may cause harm to other guests.
- Destroying the lawn or flower garden, or standing in line in an illegal way in the waiting line
- Loud singing, extreme behavior, using attractions after drinking, lying on a bench, etc.
- Smoking is allowed only in areas marked as smoking areas.
Please use the packed lunch you brought in at the picnic area.
- Boxed lunches are not allowed in the restaurant or on the outdoor terrace.
- Please refer to the on-site guidebook for the picnic area.
Please refrain from feeding the animals or taking pictures with the flash on.
Please refrain from carrying children on their shoulders as it is dangerous.
Tips for using attractions
- Please lock the safety device correctly in the designated seat and use it in the correct posture.
- Please do not carry items that are easy to fall on board.
- Please refrain from dangerous actions such as filming and eating while on board.
- Please observe the passenger safety standards according to the passenger's health condition.
- For the safety of customers and animals, the use of assistance dogs may be restricted at some attractions and some zoo facilities.
Guidelines for implementing smart queues
- When using a model equipped with smart queue, after applying for smart queue at the facility in the Everland App Available at the reserved time.
Information on implementation of boarding reservation system for the disabled
- In order to help some disabled customers who have difficulty standing in line for a long time, we are implementing the 'boarding reservation system for the disabled'. Thank you for your cooperation.
Respect workers who do their best.
- Even if there are mistakes or inadequacies, please refrain from rude language, abusive language, or physical contact.
- For suggestions, please call the manager. (Representative number: 031-320-5000)

Attraction Safety Guidebook

Information on the use of attractions for guests with disabilities

- Disabled Boarding Reservation System

Subject of use

Difficulty standing in line for a long time when using attractions Severe degree of disability (existing level 1-3) Physical/visual/mental disabilities * If it is difficult to wait for a long time due to other physical disabilities, apply after consultation with the customer service center

Companion

Disabled person + up to 3 people (no age restrictions)

How to use

  1. ① At the customer consultation room at the front gate, identification (welfare card for the disabled) and Receive a boarding reservation card after filling out the application form * For information on available attractions and reservation time for boarding
  2. ② Arrive at an available attraction and reserve a boarding time * The worker applies the same waiting time as the current waiting time, Write the boarding reservation time on the card
  3. ③ Until the reserved boarding time, use of restaurants, rest areas, and toilets; Wait comfortably in a different place you want, such as viewing
  4. ④ Present your boarding reservation card at the reserved attraction boarding time. Present it to the worker and board
* Additional waiting time may occur if there are a large number of reserved guests. * After boarding of the first reserved attraction ends, other attractions Boarding can be reserved

Issuance of boarding reservation card for the disabled / information place Customer Counseling Room (opposite the top of Sky Cruise)
