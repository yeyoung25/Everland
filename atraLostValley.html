Lost Valley

Lost Valley in Mother Nature where 150 animal friends of 20 species live together! Meet various animal friends right here.

[Information Use]
  • - As a facility operated by smart queue, depending on the operating situation It can be converted to an on-site queue.
  • - When switching to on-site queuing, the on-site queuing transition time is You can check it in the Everland App.
  • - You can use it at the reserved time after applying for smart queue. It is not available if the reservation is closed.
  • - Depending on animal health and weather conditions, the operation of attractions may change. Subject to termination and change without notice.
[Notice]
  • - Children under 36 months can board when accompanied by a guardian.
  • - Depending on the animal's health and other circumstances, it may be canceled or changed without notice.
  • - For the safety of customers and animals, the use of assistance dogs may be restricted.
  • - To prevent COVID-19, please wear a mask when boarding.
  • - It is not available for those who drink, claustrophobia, or easily get dizzy.
