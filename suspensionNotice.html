SUSPENSION NOTICE

We regularly conduct safety checks for your safety.

- Please understand that the attraction operation may be suspended in case of bad weather such as heavy rain, p winds, and low temperature.

- The holiday plan is updated on a weekly basis, so if the holiday plan for the scheduled visit date is not confirmed, Please reconfirm your holiday plan before your visit.

- Operation plans are subject to change without notice depending on the site situation.
